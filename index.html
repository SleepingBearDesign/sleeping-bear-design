Our Design Process

The Meeting

First, we meet. Contact us for a free consultation over coffee or a quick phone call to discuss what services you're looking for and how Sleeping Bear can help.

The Ideas

Next, the ideas start turning. This is where we work with you one-on-one to get the specifics of what you want and don't want. Once we have a good idea of your ideas and tastes, we'll start creating a visually aesthetic website that both you and your visitors will love.

The Product

Finally, the launch. After all tweaks and modifications are ironed out, we make your new site available to the public. After this point, you can share your page with friends, family, and customers, and you'll be able to see the immediate impact of your strong, new web presence.
